Soccer Game

How to Play:

In the soccer game, two players compete against each other, with one using the WASD keys and the other the arrow keys.
The aim is simple: be the first to collect 10 balls to score a point.
The first player to reach 3 points wins the game.
Steer skillfully, collect the balls and be the ultimate ball hunter! Become the champion of this exciting duel!
